01 — About

Quality is a design decision, not a final check.

Tristan is a QA Automation Engineer who builds testing systems that teams actually trust — frameworks that are readable, maintainable, and fast enough to run on every commit.

His work centers on Python-based automation: pytest for API and unit-level coverage, Robot Framework for keyword-driven acceptance tests that non-engineers can read and extend. He's as comfortable defining a test strategy from scratch as he is untangling a flaky suite nobody wants to touch.

Outside of test runs, he's usually deep in a new tool, refining a CI pipeline, or figuring out how to make the next suite easier to maintain than the last.
